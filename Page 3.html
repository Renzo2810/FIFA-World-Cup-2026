Stadiums

On June 16, 2022, the sixteen host cities (two in Canada, three in Mexico, eleven in the United States) were announced by FIFA: Atlanta, Boston, Dallas, Guadalajara, Houston, Kansas City, Los Angeles, Mexico City, Miami, Monterrey, New York/New Jersey, Philadelphia, San Francisco Bay Area, Seattle, Toronto, and Vancouver. These are the three biggest stadiums:
    Mexico City, Mexico – Estadio Azteca – Capacity: 87,523
    New York/New Jersey, USA – MetLife Stadium – Capacity: 82,500
    Dallas , USA – AT&T Stadium – Capacity: 80,000
